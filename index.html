digital transformation program

#internal

The English Club — a successful school of languages in Chelyabinsk, Russia (my home town) — was in need of digital transformation that would streamline all customer operations and bring them together in one place.

In 2018-2019 I led a project that involved 7 different functions and affected 5000+ customers in how they interact with The English Club. this effort has reduced the manual work of customer experience and accounting teams by half.

The project took two years from scoping, planning & selecting vendors to implementation, rollout and user adoption. It was completed on budget, and the outcomes of it have been operational ever since.
